An open Bible, verified by everyone.

OpenNeo is a peer-to-peer Scripture protocol — Neo Ontology Scripture (NeoOS), open source. Every verse is content-addressed, every correction is anchored to Bitcoin, and translators are paid in Lightning. No gatekeeper over the text.

Read now Propose a correction

Content-addressed

Every verse is hashed with BLAKE3 into one corpus root. Change a letter and the hash changes — tampering is impossible to hide.

Bitcoin-anchored

Every accepted correction is timestamped to Bitcoin. The full history is immutable, public, and independently verifiable.

Lightning-paid

When a correction merges, the work is split across translator, reviewers, and submitter — and paid over Lightning.

How it fits together

Four interlocking layers

OpenNeo is a specialization of Nostr for Scripture, with Bitcoin for permanence and Lightning for payments.

NeoOS

Neo Ontology Scripture — an open source, accuracy-first English corpus (85 of 87 books today), Hebrew names, every verse content-addressed with BLAKE3.

Relay Protocol

Content-addressed events on Nostr relays. Anyone runs a relay; clients pull from many, push to many.

Bitcoin Translation Protocol

Immutable PR history anchored to Bitcoin via OpenTimestamps, with Lightning payouts and peer review.

OpenNeo Reader

PWA + CLI: read, see change history and Bitcoin anchors, propose and vote on revisions.
