Brand guidelines

The OpenNeo brand

OpenNeo is an open protocol, and so is its identity — everything here is original and released under the project's licenses. Use it to represent OpenNeo faithfully.

Logo

The wordmark pairs a geometric N lettermark — a straight-stroke N with a small checker foot — with the OpenNeo name. Give it room; never redraw it.

Usage

Do

  • Keep the N lettermark's checker foot intact.
  • Use the accent colour for the N against neutral text.
  • Give the logo clear space on all sides.
  • Use it on either the Cream or Dark background.

Don't

  • Don't recolour the wordmark into a gradient or rainbow.
  • Don't stretch, rotate, or re-space the lettermark.
  • Don't place it on a busy or low-contrast background.
  • Don't swap the display face for another font.
